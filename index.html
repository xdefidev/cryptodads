Welcome To LoadOut!! Grab your loot and get ready for war!!

LoadOut is a Loot (For Adventures) type NFT, but is the first with Utility! Although it's just text on a screen, each LoadOut NFT will come with a Serial Number that you will be able to put into our Soldier Database. This Database will show you your Soldier's Portrait, as well as your stats from your battles in the Survival Arenas! Survival Arenas test the best soldiers in a 12 person Battle Royal Simulator using your soldier's Loadout! These Battle Royal's will have buy in's ranging from $0-$100 with prize pools paying the top 3 survivors up to $1000 per victory! There are many different factors that will affect your chances of success. From your weapon's attachment to the Arena Map your decisions are vital to your survival! There will be classes based on rarity and maps to keep Survival Arenas enjoyable for all-level soldiers. There is much more planned in the future such as, air-dropped NFT Medals for achievements in the Survival Arena and raising funds to help build schools in Jamaica. For now, we are focused on continuing to work on the Soldier Database and getting the Beta for Survival Arenas out as soon as possible for you all to start playing!
